About melody

When you work with Linked Open Data, you often want to have a quick overview of what is in your data. Get some figure from a query, maybe plot a chart, take some notes, and share with others what you found. Right?

With MELODY (Make mE a Linked Open Data storY) you can do it, and you can do it easily! You can choose among several templates for presenting your data, including charts, maps, text search (and more!) and you can export or publish your web-ready stories here

Get started

What you need is

  • know where your Linked Open Data are the URL of an endpoint
  • know how to query your data SPARQL!
  • a github account optional
  • know what story you want to tell us get inspired with some examples
