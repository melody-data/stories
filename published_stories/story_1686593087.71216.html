People and cities in MusicBo: Knowledge Graph Overview

An insight into the MusicBo corpus through knowledge extraction.

The MusicBo corpus contains 135 texts about Bologna's music heritage. The exploration of its knowledge graph by means of the Polifonia endpoint allows to retrieve data which provides a quantitative and qualitative overview of the corpus’s contents.
