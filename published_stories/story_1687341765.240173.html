Dolly Parton: The Queen of Country Music

Country music has its deep roots in Tennessee and in the Queen herself, Dolly Parton

This story aims at delineating the success of one of the greatest American singers representative of country music. Tennessee has always given birth to a great amount of singers performing many different genres. However, country music seems to be the core of this US southern state. For this reason, the main focus of this story will be the woman who is considered the Queen herself of country music, the multi-talented, Tennessee-born Dolly Parton.
