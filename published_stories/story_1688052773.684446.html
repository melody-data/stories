Exploring MusicBo Dataset

An statistical overview about people, artists and instruments

This story presents the first step of our project. It deals with the exploration of the Musicbo Knowledge database starting from the general concept of people on the one hand, and of instruments on the other hand. Concerning people we then borrowed it down to the category of artists. By consequence, the aim of these counters is e to make a comparison between the amount of data present in the database we have used: MusicBo and Wikidata.
