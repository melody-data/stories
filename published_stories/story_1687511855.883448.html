Exploring musical instruments: the violin

An overview about the violin's main features, its history and the violinists that have made the history of music

The purpose of this story is to provide a general insight of a very renowned musical instrument that has marked an important turning point in the history of music thanks to its remarkable elegance: the violin. In particular, in the following story we will observe and analyse the main features and the history of this beautiful instrument. In addition, we will also provide a general insight of the most influential and talented violinists that with their remarkable works achieved lasting fame and played a fundamental role in the history of violin music.
