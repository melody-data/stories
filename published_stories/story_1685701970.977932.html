Theatres in the UK and classical music

An insight of the theatrical form of music

Our project aims at sharing general information about theatres with a focus on UK and the most played genre in theatres: classical music. With the newer generations, theatres have lost their popularity and rarely see young people amongst their audience. With our project we hope to raise our peers' interests about theatres.
