Johann Sebastian Bach - An overview

An overview on classical 18th century composers and their impact on contemporary cultural heritage

The following story data explores the life and works of Johann Sebastian Bach with particular attention to their resonance in contemporary European culture and identity. We indeed believe that, apart from being just cultural products, cultural works (of whatever kind and of whatever genre) contributed to creating an idea of belonging and to shaping a common shared identity, a European one in our case. In music among the ones who mostly influenced the creation of a European cultural heritage was Johann Sebastian Bach. This story will be theoretically divided into two major parts: in the former we will present some bibliographical data on the composer in order to have a clear presentation on who he was, where he was born and what works he worked on; the latter, on the other hand, will deal more with the contemporary resonance that his works had (and still have under major different forms) in our culture. We will indeed have a look at all those cultural products (concerts, books, films) which may echo Johann Sebastian Bach.
