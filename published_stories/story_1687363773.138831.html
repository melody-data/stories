Måneskin

One of the most influential bands of the last 20 years

Introducing the sensational rock band Maneskin, an Italian rock band formed in 2016 that has captured the hearts of music enthusiasts worldwide, with their edgy performances and their rebellious spirit. This band has reached massive international success, especially after winning the European Song Contest in 2021, becoming the leading exporters of Italian culture all over the world. Today this group continues to redefine the boundaries of modern rock music.
