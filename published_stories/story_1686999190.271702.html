Gaspare Spontini

An 18th century Italian composer

The following story will explore the WikiData knowledge base to discover the most significant points and events in the Italian composer Gaspare Spontini’s personal and professional life. We will start with a general exploration of the WikiData knowledge base to discover the quantity of people and artists in comparison with MusicBO and then we will narrow down our research analysing composers in WikiData, until finding our main object of the story: Gaspare Spontini. In particular, we will focus on his biography, his occupations and fields of work, the awards he received during his life, where he worked and the cities where he lived. There will be at the end an insight on the Museum Gaspare Spontini, its location and the visitors he had per year.
