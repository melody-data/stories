Exploring Music Organizations in MusicBo

An inspection of MusicBo’s organizations going from a more general point of view to the discovery of specific aspects regarding academies, their members and the XVIII century music scene.

This study aims at displaying the music organizations in the MusicBo corpus. In particular, we focused on the organizations in Bologna, while uncovering the dominance of violinists within the music scene in the XVIII century.
